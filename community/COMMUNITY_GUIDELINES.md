# Community Guidelines

These guidelines help ensure a positive, productive, and inclusive environment for all members of the Ekubo Protocol EVM community.

---

## Be Respectful

- Treat everyone with respect and kindness.
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences.

## Stay On Topic

- Keep discussions relevant to Ekubo Protocol EVM and related subjects.
- Use appropriate channels for technical, governance, or general topics.

## Collaborate Constructively

- Offer helpful feedback and constructive criticism.
- Support others and share knowledge.

## No Harassment or Discrimination

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
- Report violations to maintainers or moderators.

## Protect Privacy

- Do not share personal or confidential information without consent.
- Respect the privacy of others.

## Follow the Code of Conduct

- Review and adhere to the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Use Official Channels

- Use the listed [Communication Channels](./COMMUNICATION_CHANNELS.md) for project-related discussions and support.
