# REVENUE_SHARING.md

## Detailed Revenue Sharing Terms

This document outlines the specific terms and procedures for revenue sharing under the Ekubo DAO Shared Revenue License 1.0 for the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

## License Tier Selection

Revenue share percentage depends on your chosen license tier. See [LICENSE_PURCHASE_OPTIONS.md](../LICENSE_PURCHASE_OPTIONS.md) for details.

| License Tier | Revenue Share | Annual Fee |
|--------------|---------------|------------|
| Option 1 (Default) | 50% | $0 |
| Option 2 | 25% | $50,000 |
| Option 3 | 10% | $100,000 |
| Option 4 | 5% | $125,000 |

**Default:** If no tier is purchased, Option 1 (50% revenue share) applies.

---

### 1. Revenue Sources

Revenue includes all protocol-generated fees, profits, and income from:
- Swaps and trades
- Liquidity provision
- Transaction fees
- Any other monetized protocol features

---

### 2. What Does NOT Constitute Revenue

The following are **excluded** from revenue calculations:
- User deposits (returned to users)
- Gas fees paid by users (go to miners/validators)
- Operational costs (infrastructure, development, etc.)
- Donations or grants

---

### 3. Revenue Share Percentage

Your revenue share percentage is determined by your license tier:

- **Option 1 (Default):** 50% of total protocol revenue
- **Option 2:** 25% of total protocol revenue + $50k annual fee
- **Option 3:** 10% of total protocol revenue + $100k annual fee  
- **Option 4:** 5% of total protocol revenue + $125k annual fee

---

### 4. Accepted Currencies & Chain

- **Primary:** ETH (Ethereum)
- **Secondary (if approved):** USDC, DAI
- **Chain:** Ethereum mainnet (unless otherwise approved by Ekubo DAO governance)

---

### 5. Calculation Methodology

- Revenue is calculated **quarterly**.
- Calculation is based on on-chain data and/or off-chain accounting.
- Direct transaction costs (e.g., gas fees paid by the protocol) are deducted from gross revenue.
- For detailed methodology, see [CALCULATION_METHODOLOGY.md](./CALCULATION_METHODOLOGY.md).

---

### 6. Payment Process

**Revenue Share Payments:**
- Payments must be made to the designated Ekubo DAO address:
  `0xEkuboDAORevenueAddress` (replace with actual address when available)
- Payment deadline: Within 10 business days after the end of each quarter
- Include a memo/reason for payment (e.g., "Ekubo Protocol EVM Q1 2026 Revenue Share - Option 2")
- Provide transaction hash as confirmation

**Annual License Fee Payments (Options 2, 3, 4):**
- Can be paid annually or quarterly
- Due at start of license period (or quarterly: Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- Same payment address as revenue share
- Include memo: "Ekubo Protocol EVM 2026 License Fee - Option [X]"
