## How to Make Payments to Ekubo DAO

This guide explains the steps for submitting revenue share payments to Ekubo DAO as required by the Ekubo DAO Shared Revenue License 1.0.

---

### 1. Payment Preparation

- Calculate the total revenue share owed for the reporting period (see [REVENUE_SHARING.md](./REVENUE_SHARING.md)).
- Confirm the accepted currency (ETH preferred; USDC or DAI if approved).
- Ensure you have the correct payment address for Ekubo DAO:
  - **Ekubo DAO Revenue Address:** `0x8F29e2b3c67467CF6B1920d9876e378E3B7A0595`

---

### 2. Making the Payment

- Use your wallet or protocol treasury to initiate the transfer.
- Enter the Ekubo DAO revenue address as the recipient.
- Specify the payment amount as calculated.
- Add a memo or reason for payment (e.g., "Ekubo Protocol EVM Q4 2025 Revenue Share").
- Confirm the transaction and save the transaction hash.

---

### 3. Payment Confirmation

- Record the transaction hash for reporting.
- Include the transaction hash in your quarterly revenue report.
- If required, notify Ekubo DAO via email or GitHub issue with payment details.

---

### 4. Deadlines

- Payments must be completed within 10 business days after the end of each quarter (unless otherwise approved).
