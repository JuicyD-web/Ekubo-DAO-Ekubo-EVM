# Reporting Schedule

This document outlines the deadlines for submitting revenue reports and payments to Ekubo DAO.

---

## Quarterly Reports

- **Q1:**  
  - Reporting Period: January 1 – March 31  
  - Report Due: April 15

- **Q2:**  
  - Reporting Period: April 1 – June 30  
  - Report Due: July 15

- **Q3:**  
  - Reporting Period: July 1 – September 30  
  - Report Due: October 15

- **Q4:**  
  - Reporting Period: October 1 – December 31  
  - Report Due: January 15 (following year)

---

## Annual Reports

- **Reporting Period:** January 1 – December 31  
- **Report Due:** January 15 (following year)

---

## Payment Deadlines

- Payments must be completed within 10 business days after the end of each reporting period (quarterly or annual).

---

## Submission Instructions

- Submit reports and payment proofs via GitHub issue or official DAO contact channel.
- Use the appropriate templates from `/revenue/templates/`.
