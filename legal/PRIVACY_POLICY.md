# PRIVACY_POLICY.md

## Privacy Policy for Ekubo Protocol EVM Users

This Privacy Policy explains how privacy is handled for users of the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

### 1. Data Collection

- The protocol does **not** collect personal data by default.
- All transactions and interactions are recorded on the public blockchain and are visible to anyone.

### 2. Use of Data

- Data generated by your use of the protocol (e.g., transaction history) is stored on-chain and is publicly accessible.
- No off-chain personal data is collected, stored, or processed unless explicitly stated.

### 3. Third-Party Services

- The protocol may interact with third-party services (e.g., RPC providers, block explorers).
- These services may have their own privacy policies; users should review them independently.

### 4. Security

- Users are responsible for securing their wallets, private keys, and personal devices.
- Ekubo DAO and contributors do not have access to user funds or private information.

### 5. Cookies and Tracking

- The protocol does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

### 6. Updates to Policy

- This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed.
- Changes will be posted in this file; continued use of the protocol constitutes acceptance of updates.

### 7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact: aksels.laivenieks@outlook.com
