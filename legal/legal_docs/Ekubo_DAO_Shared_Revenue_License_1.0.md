# Ekubo DAO Shared Revenue License 1.0

## Overview

This license governs the use, distribution, and modification of the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation. It ensures that all derivative works and deployments share a portion of protocol revenue with Ekubo DAO.

## Key Terms

- **Revenue Sharing:**  
  All operators and deployers of the protocol must pay 20% of all protocol-derived revenue to Ekubo DAO.

- **Compliance:**  
  Failure to comply with revenue sharing or reporting obligations results in loss of rights to use, modify, or distribute the protocol.

- **Attribution:**  
  All derivative works must credit Ekubo DAO and the original authors.

- **Modification:**  
  Only the copyright owner or designated representatives may modify or upgrade this license.

## Obligations

- **Quarterly Reporting:**  
  Operators must submit detailed revenue reports to Ekubo DAO at the end of each quarter.

- **Payment:**  
  Revenue share payments must be made to the designated Ekubo DAO address.

- **Audit:**  
  Ekubo DAO reserves the right to audit revenue calculations and payments.

## Enforcement

Ekubo DAO may revoke license rights for non-compliance, misrepresentation, or failure to pay revenue shares.
