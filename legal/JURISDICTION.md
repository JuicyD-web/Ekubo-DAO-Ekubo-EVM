# JURISDICTION.md

## Legal Jurisdiction Notes

This document provides information about the legal jurisdiction applicable to the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

### 1. Decentralized Nature

- Ekubo Protocol EVM is a decentralized software project governed by Ekubo DAO.
- There is no central legal entity or physical headquarters.

### 2. Applicable Law

- Users and operators are subject to the laws and regulations of their own jurisdiction.
- Ekubo DAO does not specify a governing jurisdiction for the protocol.

### 3. Dispute Resolution

- Disputes are resolved through the Ekubo DAO governance process (see [DISPUTE_RESOLUTION.md](../governance/DISPUTE_RESOLUTION.md)).
- Legal claims may be subject to the jurisdiction of the parties involved.

### 4. International Use

- The protocol is intended for global use.
- Users must ensure compliance with local laws, including financial, tax, and data protection regulations.

### 5. No Legal Advice

- This document does not constitute legal advice.
- Users and operators should consult legal professionals regarding jurisdictional matters.
