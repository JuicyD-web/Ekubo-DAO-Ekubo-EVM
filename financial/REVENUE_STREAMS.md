# Revenue Streams

This document lists all revenue sources for the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

## 1. Trading Fees

- Fees collected from swaps and trades executed on the protocol.

## 2. Liquidity Provider Fees (Protocol Share)

- The protocol retains a portion of fees generated by liquidity provision.

## 3. Protocol-Owned Liquidity Earnings

- Earnings from liquidity positions held directly by the protocol.

## 4. Flash Loan Fees

- Fees charged for executing flash loan transactions.

## 5. Administrative Fees

- Fees for administrative actions such as pool creation, governance proposals, or other protocol services.

## 6. Other Income

- Any additional income generated by the protocol, such as partnerships, integrations, or incentives.

---

**Excluded from Revenue:**
- User deposits (returned to users)
- Gas fees paid to the network
- Donations or grants
