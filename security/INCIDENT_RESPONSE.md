# INCIDENT_RESPONSE.md

## Incident Response Plan

This document outlines the procedures for responding to security incidents affecting the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

## 1. Detection

- Monitor for unusual activity, bug reports, or vulnerability disclosures.
- Use automated tools and community feedback for early detection.

---

## 2. Initial Assessment

- Confirm the validity and severity of the incident.
- Gather all relevant information and evidence.

---

## 3. Containment

- If necessary, pause affected contracts or disable vulnerable features.
- Notify core contributors and relevant stakeholders.

---

## 4. Investigation

- Analyze the root cause and impact of the incident.
- Review logs, transactions, and code changes.

---

## 5. Remediation

- Develop and test a fix or mitigation.
- Deploy emergency patches as needed.
- Communicate progress to affected users and the community.

---

## 6. Recovery

- Restore normal operations and verify system integrity.
- Monitor for further issues.

---

## 7. Disclosure

- Publicly disclose the incident and resolution after the issue is fixed.
- Credit reporters and provide a post-mortem analysis.

---

## 8. Documentation

- Record the incident, response actions, and outcomes in the audit log.
- Update security policies and procedures as needed.
