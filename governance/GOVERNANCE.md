# GOVERNANCE.md

## Ekubo Protocol EVM Governance Structure

This document outlines the governance structure for the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation.

---

### 1. Governance Body

- **Ekubo DAO** is the primary governing entity.
- Decisions are made by DAO members through proposals and voting.

---

### 2. Decision-Making Process

- Proposals are submitted by community members or operators.
- Proposals are discussed in the official DAO forum and/or GitHub.
- Voting is conducted using on-chain or off-chain mechanisms as determined by Ekubo DAO.
- Approved proposals are implemented by designated maintainers or contributors.

---

### 3. Areas Governed

- Protocol upgrades and changes
- Revenue sharing terms and compliance
- License updates
- Dispute resolution and appeals
- Contributor recognition and onboarding

---

### 4. Dispute Resolution

- Disputes are submitted to Ekubo DAO via the official forum or contact channel.
- The DAO reviews and votes on resolutions.
- Appeals can be made through additional proposals.

---

### 5. Communication Channels

- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Ekubo-DAO/Ekubo-EVM/issues
- Email: aksels.laivenieks@outlook.com

---

### 6. Updates to Governance

- Governance structure may be updated by DAO proposal and vote.
- Changes are documented in this file and communicated to all stakeholders.
