## Dispute Resolution Process

This document outlines the process for resolving disputes related to the Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation, including revenue sharing, compliance, governance, and contributor matters.

---

### 1. Initial Resolution

- Attempt to resolve disputes directly between involved parties through discussion and negotiation.
- Document the issue and proposed solutions.

### 2. Submission to Ekubo DAO

- If unresolved, submit the dispute to Ekubo DAO via:
  - Official DAO forum: [N/A]
  - GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Ekubo-DAO/Ekubo-EVM/issues
  - Email: aksels.laivenieks@outlook.com

- Provide all relevant details, evidence, and documentation.

### 3. DAO Review

- Ekubo DAO reviews the dispute and may request additional information.
- The dispute is discussed by DAO members in the forum or designated channel.

### 4. Voting & Decision

- DAO members vote on proposed resolutions.
- The outcome is documented and communicated to all parties.

### 5. Appeals

- If a party disagrees with the decision, they may submit an appeal via a new proposal to Ekubo DAO.
- Appeals are reviewed and voted on by DAO members.
