# LICENSE PURCHASE OPTIONS

## Ekubo Protocol EVM - Flexible Licensing Tiers

The Ekubo Protocol EVM implementation offers multiple licensing options to accommodate different business models and deployment scales. Choose the option that best fits your operational strategy.

---

## Available License Tiers

### **Option 1: Standard Revenue Share (Default)**
- **Revenue Share:** 50% to Ekubo DAO
- **Annual Fee:** $0
- **Best For:** Startups, projects with uncertain revenue, maximum flexibility
- **Terms:**
  - Quarterly revenue reporting and payment
  - No upfront costs
  - Full protocol access
  - Perpetual license as long as compliance is maintained

---

### **Option 2: Reduced Share + Annual License**
- **Revenue Share:** 25% to Ekubo DAO
- **Annual License Fee:** $50,000 USD
- **Best For:** Growing protocols with moderate revenue expectations
- **Terms:**
  - Quarterly revenue reporting and payment (25%)
  - Annual license fee paid upfront or quarterly ($12,500/quarter)
  - Renewable yearly
  - Full protocol access and updates

**Payment Schedule:**
- Annual: $50,000 due at start of license period
- Quarterly: $12,500 per quarter + 25% revenue share

---

### **Option 3: Minimal Share + Premium License**
- **Revenue Share:** 10% to Ekubo DAO
- **Annual License Fee:** $100,000 USD
- **Best For:** Established protocols with significant revenue
- **Terms:**
  - Quarterly revenue reporting and payment (10%)
  - Annual license fee paid upfront or quarterly ($25,000/quarter)
  - Renewable yearly
  - Priority support and consultation
  - Full protocol access and updates

**Payment Schedule:**
- Annual: $100,000 due at start of license period
- Quarterly: $25,000 per quarter + 10% revenue share

---

### **Option 4: Enterprise License**
- **Revenue Share:** 5% to Ekubo DAO
- **Annual License Fee:** $125,000 USD
- **Best For:** Large-scale deployments, institutional operators
- **Terms:**
  - Quarterly revenue reporting and payment (5%)
  - Annual license fee paid upfront or quarterly ($31,250/quarter)
  - Renewable yearly
  - Priority support, dedicated consultation
  - Early access to updates and features
  - Full protocol access

**Payment Schedule:**
- Annual: $125,000 due at start of license period
- Quarterly: $31,250 per quarter + 5% revenue share

## How to Select Your License Tier

1. **Estimate Your Annual Revenue:**
   - Calculate expected protocol fees and income

2. **Calculate Total Cost for Each Option:**
   - Option 1: Revenue × 50%
   - Option 2: $50,000 + (Revenue × 25%)
   - Option 3: $100,000 + (Revenue × 10%)
   - Option 4: $125,000 + (Revenue × 5%)

3. **Choose the Most Cost-Effective Option:**
   - Compare total annual costs across all tiers
   - Consider support and feature benefits

## How to Purchase a License Tier

1. **Contact Ekubo DAO:**
   - Email: aksels.laivenieks@outlook.com
   - Submit via GitHub issue with label `license-request`

2. **Provide Required Information:**
   - Selected license tier (Option 2, 3, or 4)
   - Estimated annual revenue
   - Deployment timeline
   - Contact information

3. **License Agreement:**
   - Ekubo DAO will provide a formal license agreement
   - Review terms and payment schedule
   - Sign agreement digitally

4. **Payment:**
   - Send annual or first quarterly payment to designated address
   - Provide transaction hash for confirmation
   - License becomes active upon payment confirmation

5. **Deployment:**
   - Deploy protocol using provided deployment scripts
   - Register deployment in official registry
   - Begin quarterly reporting as per selected tier

## License Renewal

- **Annual licenses expire after 12 months**
- **Renewal Notice:** Sent 60 days before expiration
- **Renewal Process:** 
  1. Review updated terms (if any)
  2. Pay renewal fee
  3. Continue operations without interruption
- **Tier Changes:** You may upgrade or downgrade at renewal

---

## Switching Between Tiers

- **Mid-Term Changes:** Available with prorated fees
- **Process:**
  1. Submit tier change request
  2. Calculate prorated difference
  3. Pay adjustment fee
  4. Update license agreement

## Compliance & Reporting

All license tiers require:
- Quarterly revenue reports (use [REVENUE_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md](./revenue/templates/REVENUE_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md))

- **Missed Payments:** 30-day grace period, then revert to Option 1 (50% share)
- **False Reporting:** License revocation, legal action
- **Serious Violations:** Public notice, loss of all license rights

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I start with Option 1 and upgrade later?**  
A: Yes, you can switch tiers at any time with prorated fee adjustments.

**Q: What happens if my revenue exceeds expectations?**  
A: You can upgrade to a higher tier mid-year to reduce revenue share percentage.

**Q: Are license fees paid to the same address as revenue share?**  
A: Yes, all payments go to the designated Ekubo DAO address.

**Q: Can I pay license fees in installments?**  
A: Yes, quarterly payments are available for all tiers.

**Q: What if I stop generating revenue?**  
A: Annual license fees are still required. Revenue share is only on actual revenue generated.
