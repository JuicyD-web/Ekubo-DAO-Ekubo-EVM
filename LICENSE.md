### **Option 4: Enterprise License**

## What You Must Do

1. **Share Revenue:** Pay the applicable revenue share percentage based on your chosen license tier
2. **Report Quarterly:** Submit detailed revenue reports every quarter
3. **Pay on Time:** Make payments within 10 business days of quarter end
4. **Maintain Attribution:** Keep copyright notices and license references
5. **Comply with Terms:** Follow all requirements in the full LICENSE

## What Revenue Counts

**Included:**
- Trading fees collected by the protocol
- Liquidity provider fees (protocol share)
- Flash loan fees
- Any protocol-generated income

**Excluded:**
- User deposits (returned to users)
- Gas fees (paid to miners/validators)
- Operational costs
- Donations or grants
