# FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions for Ekubo Protocol EVM Implementation

---

## General

**Q: What is Ekubo Protocol EVM?**  
A: It is an EVM-compatible implementation of the Ekubo Protocol, supporting concentrated liquidity AMM features.

**Q: Who governs the protocol?**  
A: Ekubo DAO governs the protocol through proposals and voting.

---

## Revenue & Compliance

**Q: How is revenue shared?**  
A: 20% of protocol revenue is paid to Ekubo DAO, as described in [REVENUE_SHARING.md](../REVENUE_SHARING.md).

**Q: What counts as protocol revenue?**  
A: Trading fees, protocol share of liquidity provider fees, flash loan fees, and other income. See [REVENUE_STREAMS.md](../financial/REVENUE_STREAMS.md).

**Q: How do I report revenue?**  
A: Use the templates in `/revenue/templates/` and submit reports as described in [reporting_schedule.md](../revenue/automation/reporting_schedule.md).

## Governance

**Q: How are disputes resolved?**
